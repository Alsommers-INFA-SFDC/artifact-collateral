The Shift: Insight vs. Action

Agents do not possess human hesitation. If fed fragmented data, they will confidently take the wrong action. See how context changes the trajectory of a high-value customer interaction.

Scenario A: Fragmented Data

Agent sees: "A standard passenger with a delay."

"I've rebooked you on the next flight in 14 hours. Have a nice day."
RESULT: CUSTOMER CHURN RISK

Scenario B: Unified Data (C360)

Agent sees: "Platinum Member, baggage delay last week, prefers aisle."

"I've booked you on a partner flight in 2 hours, upgraded you to First Class (Aisle), and issued a lounge pass to compensate for your recent baggage issue."
RESULT: LIFETIME VALUE INCREASE
